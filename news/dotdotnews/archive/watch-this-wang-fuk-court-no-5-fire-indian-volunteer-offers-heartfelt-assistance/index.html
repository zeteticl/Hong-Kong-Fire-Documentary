Watch This | Wang Fuk Court No. 5 fire: Indian volunteer offers heartfelt assistance

Video
2025.11.28 18:46
Share to Wechat
Scan the QR code to share

A deadly No. 5 fire broke out at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po, resulting in multiple casualties. Various sectors of Hong Kong society have acted swiftly, spontaneously organizing assistance and actively donating money and goods, uniting to create a powerful force for disaster relief, bringing warmth and support to affected residents.

At around 12 pm, Dino and his companion, Lavina, arrived in front of the disaster-stricken building at Wang Fuk Court to pray for all the affected residents, children, and pets.

Dino is an Indian resident living in Hong Kong. He was born and raised in Hong Kong and had lived in Australia for twenty years before returning to settle in Hong Kong, which he considers his home.

Dino lives on Hong Kong Island and specifically took an hour to rush over. "Time is given," he said, "It does not matter how far we've come, each and every step counts."
