Watch This | Wang Fuk Court No. 5 fire: HK residents unite in mutual support

Video
2025.11.27 19:00
Share to Wechat
Scan the QR code to share

A deadly No. 5 fire broke out at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po, resulting in multiple casualties. Various sectors of Hong Kong society have acted swiftly, spontaneously organizing assistance and actively donating money and goods, uniting to create a powerful force for disaster relief, bringing warmth and support to affected residents.

Reporters from DotDotNews observed at the temporary shelter near the fire scene that the area was filled with various supplies, including warm clothing suitable for men, women, and children, daily necessities, fruits, food, and women's products. Displaced residents continuously came to collect supplies, and citizens consistently brought in various items. Everyone was helping each other, and the atmosphere was orderly, providing a touch of comforting solace amidst this unsettling emergency.
