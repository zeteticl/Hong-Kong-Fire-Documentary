    Residents' Discussion Records

    This section archives discussions, mutual aid information, and community voices from Wang Fuk Court residents during and after the fire. These records are valuable for reconstructing the event, understanding residents' needs, and documenting community mutual aid.

    Overview

    We are currently curating resident discussions from various channels, including:

    • Social Media
      • Wang Fuk Court Residents Facebook Group: Discussions on fire incident and maintenance issues
    • Instant Messaging Groups: Mutual aid messages from WhatsApp, Telegram owner/resident groups
    • Offline Discussions: Records from residents' meetings and concern group meetings

    (Content is being curated and will be updated gradually)

    Submit Information

    If you are a resident of Wang Fuk Court and would like to provide relevant information or discussion records, please contact us via:

    • Submit Info / Contact Us

    Note: All published discussion records will be anonymized to protect personal privacy.
